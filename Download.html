Payment Policy
Sunday, April 02, 2023
The following terms and conditions of the school fee payments apply to all pupils at Flying Wings Academy in 2023. These terms and conditions and the fees are subject to review by the School Administrative Board. Generally, fees are reviewed annually.
Application Fee
This payment is due when parents complete a formal application for a place at our school for their child. It is a once-off fee and is non-refundable. The fee is applicable for each application.
b.Miscellaneous Charges
The cost of other items such as uniforms, stationery, trips, exam fees, medical expenses etc., paid on behalf of a child, will be charged to the account and are payable on presentation.
c.School fees
This is a fee that covers all the costs related to a child receiving educational services at the school. This payment can be made in cash or a cash deposit in the mobile account of Airtel or MTN of the contact numbers given. This payment should be made before the beginning of the month paid for or term, but no later than the 10th of each month.
The school fees for the kindergarten section shall be ZMK 100.00 per month, or K 300.00 per term. Parents who are unable to pay the full term’s School fees by the due date, must please communicate this to the Accounts Department before the due date.
For parents who want their children to have lunch and breakfast prepared at school, they will have to pay a fee of ZMK 100.00 per child per month.
A full term’s written notice is required when withdrawing a child from the school. If such notice is not received, the school will charge a term’s fees in lieu of notice. In case of instances where a child misses class due to parents taking them out without being withdrawn, a maximum of one term without losing their place in the respective class is permitted. In such instances, 25% of the School Fees must be paid, irrespective of the length of the period of absence.

Assessment Policy
Sunday, April 02, 2023
The school shall ensure that all assessments are conducted on time and are of standard for all learners. The assessments shall be two; the mid-term and the end of term assessments. Both assessments are to be printed and recorded in the learners’ report books. The parents are therefore required to collect these results twice in a term. The mid-term shall be given in the mid-week of the term and the end term in the last two weeks. It is mandatory that all parents come in person or send a family representative to collect these results. Failure to do so will attract a fine of ZMK 50.00.
All the results shall be recorded in a report book that shall comprise of all the assessments in that particular year. These book shall be bought at the school at a fee of ZMK 40.00 only. The report books shall be given to parents at the close of every assessment and they are required to return the books to the school two weeks before the next assessment. If the parents damage the book, they shall be charged a fine of ZMK 50.00 for the purchase of another report book.

Learning Materials Policy
Sunday, April 02, 2023
The school shall provide all the learning materials needed to equip learners with the information they need. As for the parents, each child should be provided with the right exercise books; for the Kindergarten, 6 small books will suffice. As for the Lower Primary Section, 8 big hard covered books. The Upper Primary Section will require 10 big hard covered books.

As for the Kindergarten Section, all the books will be kept at school during the week to be given to the parents on Fridays apart from the Home Work books.

On Mondays, the parents are to pack all the books of the learners neatly and ensure that they are clean and not torn apart or worn off in any way. If however, a child reports on Monday with books missing or missing pages, the parents will have to replace them before the end of that particular day.

Enrolment Policy
Sunday, April 02, 2023
As a school we are enrolling learners from Kindergarten Section to Grade 5 as early as 2 years old. The enrollment process will require a payment of ZMK 20.00 for the Registration Form which is non-refundable.

If a child is registered, they are required to report within two weeks. If the child does not report within the stipulated period, the enrolment shall be nullified, implying that the parent will have to re-register the child at the aforementioned non-refundable fee.

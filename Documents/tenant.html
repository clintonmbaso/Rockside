Caretaker’s Tenancy Agreement

Tenancy Agreement

This is a Short-Assured Tenancy within the meaning of the Housing Act of Zambia.

Section A: Parties and Premises

The Landlord: Rockside Seventh-Day Adventist Church

Landlord Address: Kabwe East Mission District

Landlord Phone: _______________________

Tenant Name(s): _______________________

The Accommodation Let Is: Church Premises (Hereafter referred to as the PROPERTY)

Commencement: ____________

End Date: TILL FURTHER NOTICE (2 months written notice required by either party)

The tenant agrees to the attached inventory and to replace or repair any missing/damaged contents, excluding fair wear and tear.

The Caretaker is responsible for electricity, water, and sewerage charges related to personal use.

The Church insures the building and its property. The tenant is responsible for insuring personal belongings.

Section B: Use of the Premises

The Caretaker agrees to use the premises as their primary residence and not for illegal or business purposes without written permission.

The Caretaker must inform the Church if absent for more than 14 days and secure the premises appropriately.

All Church properties must be accounted for and properly handled. Items must not be lent without the Head Deacon/ess’s permission.

Premises must be kept clean in liaison with Church Deacons.

The Caretaker agrees Not to:

  • Assign or sublet the premises
  • Take in lodgers or allow others to share occupancy without consent

The Caretaker agrees to:

  • Keep the premises clean and hazard-free
  • Not make unauthorized alterations or decorations
  • Maintain communal areas and avoid property damage
  • Dispose of rubbish appropriately
  • Avoid causing disturbance or harassment
  • Not keep pets without written consent
  • Allow access for inspection/repairs with 24-hour notice or immediately in emergencies

Section C: Undertakings

The Church agrees to maintain the premises in habitable condition, including structure, water, sanitation, and provided furnishings.

Repairs for defects caused by the Caretaker’s actions or negligence are the tenant’s responsibility.

The Caretaker must report any deterioration or damage immediately.

Section D: Ending the Tenancy

The Church may terminate the agreement with a notice to quit at the end of the term or upon material breach.

If the tenant does not vacate, legal action may be taken to regain possession.

General

The Caretaker must follow all other rules provided by the Church, including any additional annexures signed by both parties.

Section E: Notice & Declarations

By signing this agreement and occupying the premises, the Caretaker:

  • Confirms understanding that this is a Short-Assured Tenancy
  • Declares that all disclosures are true and complete
  • Has not made any false or misleading statements
